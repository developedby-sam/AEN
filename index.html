About AEN

The Astronomy Enthusiasts in Nepal (AEN) is a community of youths initiated non-profit organization that serves a purpose of fostering a inovative and collaborative approach to spreading astronomy knowledge across Nepal guided by the vision "Lets's Explore Togehter".

Founded in September, 2020.

AEN aims to connect people of any age for flourishing their love of astronomy and with time aiding in the growth of the astronomical community in Nepal.

Scientific Talks

We organize scientific talks on different fields of astronomy with the collaboration of professors of renowned university and other renowned personalities working in the field of astronomy

Online Courses

Our online courses are intuitive and knowledge grasping. Our online courses varies from building basic concepts in different fields of astronomy to some advanced concepts.

Scientific Debates

If you love science and debates both, well here is the platform for you. We organize timely debates based on different controversial aspects of science and astronomy.
